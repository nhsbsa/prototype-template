Based on what you've told us You get help with NHS costs

Because you get Pension Credit Guarantee Credit:

You get free:

  • ✅ NHS prescriptions
  • ✅ NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • ✅ sight tests
  • ✅ NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get money off:

  • ✅ new glasses or contact lenses
  • ✅ repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • ✅ travel for NHS treatment

    • If you were referred for this treatment by a GP, hospital doctor, dentist or optician


    Any children under 20 included on your Pension Credit Guarantee Credit claim are also entitled to the same help.

    If you are pregnant or have children

    • As part of the Healthy Start scheme, if you're at least ten weeks pregnant or have a child under the age of four, you may be entitled to:

  • ✅ £3.10 a week to spend on foods such as cow’s milk, baby formula, fresh or frozen fruit and vegetables
  • ✅ free vitamins

    • Find out more about who qualifies for Healthy Start

    You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

    • ❌ travel for NHS treatment

    You may still get help if your parent or guardian is on certain benefits or has applied for help with NHS costs.

    How to get your help

    In most circumstances you won't have to do anything as your date of birth will be on your medical records and will show your age.

    You may have to show proof that you are in full time education, like a recent letter from school or college.

    To get your free prescriptions

    Tick box B (second box down) on the back of the prescription form.
